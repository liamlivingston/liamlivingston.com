Building process

The batteries arrived on 9/13 and I spent about 2 weeks taking them apart, balaning them, and building the battery. I build the battery in a triangle shape so it would fit inside of a triangle frame bag in the middle of my bike frame. The motor installation was very easy compared to my v1 ebike and it took only around an hour. I finished building this bike on 9/28 and rode it to school the next day. I overheated the motor on 10/9 and bought a replacement motor and installed it on 10/23.
