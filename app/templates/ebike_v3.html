Batteries

I build a 72v 9ah(691.2wh) battery using LiFePO4 cells for this ebike


I also used the 60v battery from my v2 ebike for extra range on longer rides.



Frame

For the frame, I bought a stealth bomber clone from AliExpress for a bit over $300 after taxes and shipping.

Front suspension fork - SR Suntour XCM 30 29in(Out of stock where i got them for around $80 total)
Rear shock - 210mm Cheap amazon coil one for like $50
Brakes - Zoom HB-876
Front wheel - 29in MTB Front Wheel Disc Brakes
Headset -
Handlebars -


Motor Kit

For ebike v3.1 I used the same motor kit as my v2 ebike because the new motor I ordered took about 2 months to ship. I hit a top speed of around 40mph with the 72v LFP battery and around 35mph with my 60v battery.


Upgrades v3.2

For v3.2, I upgraded to a QS205 50H V3 3.5t motor on a 29in mtx rim. For the controller, I used a VECTOR 200 from Golden Motor because I was able to borrow one from my solar car team.

This increased the top speed to around 55mph but only improved the acceleration a bit.

Future Plans

I'm going replace the controller with one that is designed for a hub motor as the VECTOR 200 was designed for a different type of motor. I'm also going to install a motorcycle style seat and create a external battery mount on it to mount my upgraded originally 60v and now 72v battery.
